📋 FILTER CAPABILITIES
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

  /api/exercises/filter/?
    exercise_type=1          ← By type (Strength, Cardio, etc.)
    muscle_group=1           ← By muscle group
    muscle=5                 ← By specific muscle
    equipment=1,2,3          ← By equipment
    location=home            ← home, gym, or both
    difficulty=beginner      ← beginner, intermediate, advanced
    exclude_injured=true     ← Exclude injured muscles

  Examples:
  • /api/exercises/filter/?exercise_type=1&location=gym
  • /api/exercises/filter/?difficulty=beginner&location=home
  • /api/exercises/filter/?muscle_group=1&exclude_injured=true
